About Me

A Mini-Biography of Loren Bostic

My name is Loren. I am an aspiring photographer, security nerd, and wanderlust adventurer. I am originally from southern New Hampshire but I also have Texan roots. I am currently situated in Boston, Massachusetts, where technology and photography truly intersect to allow for me to capture picturesque moments. On the side, I love to travel, paddle-board, run, and enjoy life. Most importantly, I love a good challenge whether it is testing new photography software or getting the perfect set of colors in a photograph. Otherwise, I am always looking forward to capturing all kinds of moments in photographs.
